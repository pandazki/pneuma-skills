# 冻结内核 / Frozen kernel

> 这是这篇文章**不可改的东西** —— 论点、事实、精确的让步。WordTaste 在改写时会逐条让它们在成稿里活着，
> 但出场的顺序和方式可以重排。结构、纹理、体裁、比喻都能动；**内核永不破。**
> This is what must NOT change — the load-bearing claims, facts, and careful hedges. WordTaste keeps every
> item alive in the rewrite (reordered and re-expressed freely), but never alters their meaning.
> Structure, texture, genre, and metaphors are all fair game to disrupt; **the kernel is invariant.**
>
> intake 时由 WordTaste 从你的原稿里抽出来填好，再请你过一眼确认。你也可以手工增删。
> WordTaste fills this in at intake from your original draft, then asks you to confirm. Edit freely.

---

## 核心论点 / Load-bearing claims
*成稿里必须站得住的判断，每条一句话。The judgments the final piece must defend, one line each.*

- 

## 事实 / 例子 / 引用 / Facts · examples · citations
*不能改错、不能丢的具体事实与例子。Concrete facts and examples that must stay accurate and present.*

- 

## 精确的让步 / 对冲 / Precise hedges
*那些「不是全盘否定 X」「在这个范围内才成立」的精确边界 —— 删了文章就会变成过度断言。*
*The careful qualifications that keep the piece from overclaiming — drop these and it becomes a hot take.*

- 

## 可以放手破的（非内核）/ Free to disrupt (NOT kernel)
*结构、小标题、比喻、体裁、节奏 —— 这些都不在内核里，WordTaste 可以大胆重排甚至推倒。*
*Structure, headings, metaphors, genre, rhythm — none of this is kernel; WordTaste may freely rebuild it.*

- 结构 / structure
- 比喻 / metaphors
- 体裁 / genre
- 节奏与段落长短 / pacing & paragraphing
