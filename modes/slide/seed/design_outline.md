# Pneuma Slide — Welcome Demo Deck

## Design Goals
- **Purpose:** Onboarding deck for first-time Pneuma Slide users
- **Audience:** Developers, designers, knowledge workers discovering the tool
- **Tone:** Warm, confident, inspiring — human creativity meets AI power
- **Key Message:** "Pneuma Slide transforms how you create presentations — describe your vision, see it come alive"
- **Style Direction:** Humanistic-tech fusion — warm cream/beige canvas, burnt orange accents, clean typography, organic visual elements

## Visual Style
- **Color Scheme:** Warm light mode
  - Background: warm cream (#FAF7F2)
  - Text: deep charcoal (#2D2A26)
  - Primary: burnt orange (#D4632A)
  - Accent: warm gold (#C4963A)
  - Surface: soft ivory (#F0EBE3)
  - Border: warm gray (#DDD5CA)
  - Muted: warm gray text (#8A8178)
- **Typography:** Inter for headings, clean sans-serif body
- **Visual Elements:** Inline SVG icons, geometric accent shapes, gradient overlays
- **Density:** Spacious — let content breathe

## Slide Structure (11 slides)

1. **Cover** — "Pneuma Slide" title + tagline
